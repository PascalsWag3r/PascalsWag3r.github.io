Hello, welcome to my website. There are various writings that I'm currently planning on completing and uploading here, for now the website may not have many pages.

Plans for the future:

  1. Anime/LN Commentaries: I break down some interesting anime or light novel series and discuss possible author intent and opinion shown through various elements of the story. I try, and will try, to do this using what I believe to be a likely correct interpretation. That is, I try my hardest to not overly read into what the author says or the anime portrays. This will hopefully be more clear with the first commentary.
  2. Five Minutes or Less: I write a short opinion piece that tries to raise and prove a certain point in less than five minutes of reading time. These pieces are not subject bound, and I may make some that aren't opinion based at all.
  3. More fun stuff to come...
